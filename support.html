Common questions

How do I back up my notebook?

Long-press the date stamp at the top of the page to open the back cover, then tap Back it up. Your notebook exports as a single file you can keep anywhere (email it to yourself, drop it in a cloud drive, etc.).

I bought the unlock but it's locked on my new phone.

Open the unlock screen and tap “already bought it? restore.” Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID, so it comes right back — no second charge.

How do I move my notebook to a new phone?

Back it up on the old phone (above), send yourself the file, then use Restore from a backup file in the back cover on the new phone.

How do I delete my data?

Delete any entry or photo right in the app, or delete the app to remove everything. Nothing is stored anywhere but your phone, so there's nothing to request from me.

Is my data private?

Yes — REPBOOK has no accounts, no analytics, and no servers. Everything stays on your device. Full details in the privacy policy.
