# REPBOOK Privacy Policy

*Last updated: July 11, 2026*

REPBOOK is a workout notebook. Your data is yours.

## What we collect

Nothing. REPBOOK has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no ads, and no
servers. We never see your data.

## What stays on your device

Everything you put in the app is stored only on your phone, in the app's
private storage:

- Workout entries (exercises, weights, reps, notes, page titles)
- Body weight numbers you jot down
- Photos you choose to add from your photo library (the app saves its own
  compressed copy; it never modifies your photo library)

## Photo library access

REPBOOK asks for photo library access only when you tap the photo button,
and only to copy the pictures you pick onto that day's page. Photos are
never uploaded anywhere.

## Backups

You can export your notebook as a single backup file. That file is created
on your device and handed to the sharing options you choose (email, cloud
drive, etc.). Where you send it is entirely up to you; REPBOOK never
transmits it itself.

## Data deletion

Delete any entry or photo in the app, or delete the app to remove
everything. Since we hold no copies, there is nothing to request from us.

## Changes

If a future version ever changes any of this (for example, optional cloud
backup), this policy will be updated before that version ships.
